Overview

In business , Many times It require to know customer Feedback, their individual product stratification level, their product need, customer like/dislikes and many more. In odoo there is survey module where we can create survey with question/answer. To Define a survey on customer there is no any such possibility so this app will integrated with the survey module to allow you to define Survey for Customer.

This Module will add functionality to define survey on customer screen and also user can create multiple survey for same customer and print customer survey answer as well.

Features

Define a survey on customer Screen
Multiple Survey for one customer
Send survey Invitation mail to customer mailbox
Print Survey Feedback on customer screen
Multiple survey history on customer screen

How To Work

Customer form

From "Update survey data " Select your created survey to fill in into customer screen

Survey Wizard

Choose your survey from window and then update survey on customer screen or send invitation mail to customer by "Set and Send Survey" button

Survey Button Display

Once Survey will load then user can start the survey of customer

Start Survey

Survey History Display

Multiple survey history on customer screen

Customer From

User can print survey by "Print Survey" button

Printed survey with Question/ Answear

Send Survey To customer

Survey Invitation on customer mailbox
